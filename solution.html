Solution statement

A web application to provide:
  • Individuals a genuine connection in the community.
  • Individuals tailored events and content.
  • Businesses a more effective method of advertising to the right audience.
  • Local clubs and groups a location/makerspace and tools/resources for meetings.

Our solution will:
  • Increase community support in finding relevant extracurricular activities, hobbies and resources
  • Generate suggestions such as makerspace and events based on groupâ€™s location and interest
  • Simplify and reduce the number of steps for finding groups.
  • Facilitate user feedback
  • Be accessible as a web application on various devices.

Our solution will not:
  • Share user information to businesses or events
  • Promote external/irrelevant ads
  • RSVP events for users
  • Help with dating or finding love (i.e. matchmaking)
